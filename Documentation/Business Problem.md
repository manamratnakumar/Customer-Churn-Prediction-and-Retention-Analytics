Business Problem

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges faced by subscription-based businesses. Losing existing customers directly impacts revenue and increases customer acquisition costs.
The objective of this project is to analyze customer behavior, identify the factors influencing churn, predict customers who are likely to leave, and provide actionable business 
recommendations to improve customer retention.


Business Objectives

Primary Objective
Predict customers who are likely to churn.

Secondary Objectives
Understand why customers leave.
Identify high-risk customer segments.
Measure revenue loss due to churn.
Recommend retention strategies.
Build an executive dashboard for management.
